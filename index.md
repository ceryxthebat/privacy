# Privacy Policy for Snack Bandit

_Last updated: May 27, 2026_

**Short version:** Snack Bandit does not collect, store, transmit, or share
any personal data. We never see anything about you.

## What we collect

Nothing. The app makes no network calls, has no analytics, no advertising,
no accounts, no sign-ins, and no third-party SDKs. Snack Bandit runs
entirely offline on your device.

## What's stored on your device

Your game progress (scraps, owned generators, upgrades, prestige count,
lifetime stats) and your sound-mute preference are saved locally using
iOS's standard storage APIs. None of this ever leaves your device.
Uninstalling the app removes all of it.

## Children

Because Snack Bandit collects no data of any kind from anyone, it complies
with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the
equivalent rules in other jurisdictions automatically. The app is rated
4+ and is appropriate for all ages.

## Third parties

None. No advertising networks, no analytics providers, no cloud services,
no crash reporters. Just you and the raccoon.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be
revised. Because we collect no data, any future changes will be cosmetic
or clarifying rather than affecting your privacy.
